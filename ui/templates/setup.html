OZY2 is an AI agent, not just a chatbot.

OZY2 is local-first. Your API keys, conversations and files never leave your machine or reach our servers. Your data is stored in ~/.ozy2/.

Always review important outputs before sending or publishing. The authors of OZY2 are not responsible for any unintended actions taken by the AI agent.
